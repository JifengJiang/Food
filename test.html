ABOUT EATRIGHT



EATRIGHT is an application that allow students to learn about healthy food choices on campus. With access to all the nutritional information of all food for each campus, our app provides students with a diet plan that allows them to eat healthier.

The diet plans are all designed for student goals and all food options that are presented are either on campus or very near campus.This allows students to use meal plan dollars to purchase and we also have a feature on the app allows students to view their meal plan balance so they can budget accordingly.
